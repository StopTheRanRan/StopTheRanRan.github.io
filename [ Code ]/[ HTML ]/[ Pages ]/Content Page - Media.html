Media:

Film

As part of my masters course, one of the assignments was to create a documentary, as part of a group. Together we planned and organised the topic, interviewees, locations, scripting, equipment, filming and editing of the documentary. We resulted in a short film surrounding the beavers living on the River Avon, interviewing a specialist who found the evidence they were initially there, and a specialist worker of the habitat who discussed how suitable the environment is for them. Even managing to canoe down the river with the specialist who helped us find and record the beavers living there.

Radio

Again during my masters course I took part in a group assignment, this one as a live radio piece recorded in the BBC studios in Bristol. We formed the topic, script and were all in person there during recording. We decided our theme to be about obsession and based our discussions throughout the show around this. I took the lead on a prerecorded package and interviewed a psychologist regarding OCD, editing the recording down to fit within the programme and helped add it to the scheduling for the as-live show. I also helped organise and set up the as-live interview.

Another project was to create a package to fit within a radio show of our choosing, and I was lucky enough to interview another scientist regarding relationships between young people and digital gaming cultures. I chose the topic, wrote the script and interview questions, and edited the package all independently.
