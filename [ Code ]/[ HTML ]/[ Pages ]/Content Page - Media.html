Media:

Film

An opportunity I was so lucky to be able to partake in was creating a documentary for my master's course In this group project we planned and organised the topic, interviewees, locations, scripting, equipment, filming and editing of the documentary by ourselves.

This hard work resulted in a short film surrounding beavers living on the River Avon, we were so fortunate with the chance to interview a specialist who initially found evidence of the beavers living in the area, as well as a specialist worker of the habitat who discussed the suitability of the area.

We even managed to organise canoeing down the river with one of the specialists, and on this trip we located and recorded our own sighting of a beaver!

Radio

Another group project during my master's course was an "as-live" radio show recorded at the BBC studios in Bristol. Together the group created the show's theme, collaborated on the script, and we all attended for the recording; to organise the scheduling and work the broadcasting desk.

We decided our theme to be about obsession and based our discussions throughout the show around this. I took the lead on a prerecorded package and interviewed a psychologist regarding OCD, editing the recording down to fit within the programme. I also helped organise and set up the live interview as this was via phone call.

Another project was to create a package to fit within a radio show of our choosing, and I was lucky enough to interview another scientist regarding relationships between young people and digital gaming cultures. I chose the topic, wrote the script alongside the interview questions, and edited the package all independently.
