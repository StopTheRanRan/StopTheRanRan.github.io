Reslife

Another opportunity where I was engaging with many different people was in the role of resident assistant, running events for university halls. In this position I connected to a variety of students, mostly those pursuing postgraduate degrees. It was fascinating to see the passions of so many different people and understand their interests. As well as that I was able to give them spaces to pursue hobbies and relax in the face of stressful experiences. I ran varying types of events ranging from arts and crafts such as acrylic pours, life drawing, and vision boards; game nights such as parties with limbo and board games, to murder mysteries; as well as sports and excursions such as cycling around Bournemouth, bus trips to the sea sauna and a colour run.
